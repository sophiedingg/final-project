The Club Above

This year we re-opened the Club Above, offering live music, DJ’s, and interactive entertainment several nights a week. Fully remodeled, with a new sound system and new dance floor, Club Above re-joins the local scene with a fresh vibe and fresh eye on providing an alternative to Ann Arbor’s surprisingly limited number of downtown places to dance and drink. See the club’s website for more in-depth information.

Book An Event

On nights when there is no entertainment booked, the space may be available for your private or public event. It’s also available during the day, and is a surprisingly vibrant space, thanks to the skylights and dynamic color scheme. Use the contact form on the right to contact us, or learn more about booking the space on the club’s website.
